🚀 Telco Customer Churn & Revenue Analysis | Data Analytics Project

Excited to share my latest end-to-end Data Analytics project, where I analyzed telecom customer 
data to understand churn behavior, revenue patterns, and customer retention trends.

The objective of this project was to transform raw telecom data into actionable business insights
that help telecom companies identify high-risk customers and improve retention strategies.

📊 Key Insights from the Analysis

• Total Revenue Analyzed: $21.37M
• Overall Churn Rate: 26.54%
• Month-to-Month Contracts show the highest churn: 45.84%
• Senior Citizens churn rate: 41.68% compared to 23.61% for others
• Highest churn risk occurs in the first 20 months (~43%) of customer tenure
• Competitor offers and service dissatisfaction are major churn drivers

This project demonstrates how data analytics can uncover churn drivers, identify high-risk customers, and support data-driven retention strategies.
