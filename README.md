# 📊 Telco Customer Churn & Revenue Analysis

## 🚀 Business Problem

Telecom companies face significant revenue loss due to **customer churn**.
This project analyzes customer data to identify **churn drivers, high-risk segments, and revenue impact**, enabling better retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Identify customers likely to churn
* Analyze revenue distribution and churn impact
* Understand behavior across contract types and tenure
* Provide actionable insights to improve retention

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* SQL
* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* Excel
* Power BI

---

## ⚙️ Approach

* Cleaned and transformed telecom dataset
* Performed **exploratory data analysis (EDA)**
* Analyzed churn patterns across multiple dimensions
* Built an interactive **Power BI dashboard** for insights

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Total Revenue Analyzed: **$21.37M**
* Overall Churn Rate: **26.54%**
* **Month-to-Month contracts** show highest churn (**45.84%**)
* **Senior Citizens churn rate: 41.68%** vs 23.61% for others
* Highest churn occurs within **first 20 months (~43%)**
* Churn strongly influenced by **competitor offers and service dissatisfaction**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

* Churn Rate by Contract Type
* Churn Risk by Customer Tenure
* Revenue Distribution by City
* Customer Segmentation by Payment Method
* Internet Service Impact on Churn
* Monthly Charges vs Churn Status

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Identifies **high-risk customer segments** for targeted retention
* Helps reduce churn through **contract and pricing strategy optimization**
* Supports **data-driven decision making** for customer retention
* Highlights early-stage churn risk for proactive engagement
